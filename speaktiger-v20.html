Arrivals

Round 1 · Arrivals

The rules

Start with the basics so everyone can join in easily.
No need to impress anyone.
Just give the table a first sense of who you are.

Step 1
📱 The phone owner starts
  • On the next screen you will have a question
  • Answer it. Keep it simple and light
Step 2
🔄 Pass it clockwise
  • Each person takes their turn
  • You'll have a few minutes ⏱️
  • Click Finish when everyone is done

Round 2 · How it works

The rules

This round is about first impressions.
You'll make a little guess about the person next to you.

Step 1
📱 Phone owner starts — you're Player A
  • What impression does the person on your left give you? Choose one option and explain.
Step 2
💬 Player B, was Player A right?
Step 3
💬 Player B, what is your impression of the next person?
Continue clockwise until everyone has guessed and responded.
